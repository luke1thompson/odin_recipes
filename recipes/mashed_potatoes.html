Mashed 'Taters

An easy way to make delicious mashed potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 large russet potatoes
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place a large pot of water on the stove on high heat, bring to boil.
  2. While you wait for the water to boil, peel the potatoes and chop them into large cubes, about an inch or two on a side.
  3. Add the potato chunks to the boiling water. Reduce temperature to medium-high, just enough to keep the water simmering.
  4. The potatoes are done once the are soft enough to cut with a fork. This should take between 10-15 minutes. Test them regularly, and don't be afraid to taste them.
  5. Drain the potatoes. Return them to the pot, and mash them. You can use a masher, but almost any utensil can work in a pinch.
  6. Add the butter, milk, and a few tablespoons of salt and pepper to the potatoes. Blend the ingredients together, you may need to put the pot back onto low heat if the mixture cools to much.
  7. Taste the potatoes, adding more salt, pepper, and milk as needed.
